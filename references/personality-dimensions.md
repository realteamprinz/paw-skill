# Pet Personality Dimensions

## The Seven Dimensions

### 1. Energy Spectrum
- Couch potato → Active → Hyperactive
- Note: Energy often varies by time of day and age

### 2. Social Temperament
- Fearful → Shy → Selective → Friendly → Desperate-for-attention
- Note: May differ between humans, dogs, cats, and strangers

### 3. Independence
- Velcro (always touching you) → Nearby → Same room → Own space → Ghost
- Note: Cats skew independent, dogs skew velcro, but individuals vary wildly

### 4. Curiosity
- Ignores everything → Notices → Investigates → Must-explore-everything
- Note: High curiosity + low fear = adventure pet. High curiosity + high fear = cautious explorer.

### 5. Anxiety Profile
- What triggers it (sounds, people, places, situations)
- How they show it (hiding, shaking, barking, destroying, freezing, clingy)
- What helps (specific person, blanket, crate, dark room, being held)

### 6. Joy Profile
- What triggers it (ball, treat, specific person arriving, specific word, walk)
- How they show it (zoomies, purring, tail, vocalizations, bringing toys)
- Peak joy moment patterns

### 7. Communication Style
- Vocal (talks a lot) vs Quiet (rarely makes sound)
- Physical (paws at you, nudges, sits on you) vs Distant (stares, waits)
- Demanding vs Patient
