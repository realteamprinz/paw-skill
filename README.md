## What is paw.skill?

An AI agent skill that distills your pet's personality from your data — photos, videos, voice recordings, your messages about them, vet records — and builds a living profile you can interact with.

Not a chatbot pretending to be your dog.
A real distillation of who they are.

## How It Works

1. **Feed it data** — Photos, videos, audio clips, text descriptions, vet records
2. **It builds a profile** — Personality, communication patterns, routines, relationships, memories
3. **Interact** — Talk to your pet through the agent. It responds based on their real personality.
4. **It learns** — Every new input deepens the profile. Self-learning, cumulative, never forgets.

## What Data Can I Feed It?

| Data Type | What It Extracts |
|---|---|
| Photos | Posture, expression, setting, mood, physical changes over time |
| Videos | Movement patterns, energy level, social behavior, play style |
| Voice recordings | Vocalization dictionary — what different sounds mean |
| Your messages | How you describe them, stories, memories, milestones |
| Vet records | Health timeline, medications, conditions |
| Social posts | Public memories, comments from people who knew your pet |

## Profile Dimensions

Every pet profile covers:

- **Identity** — Name, breed, origin story
- **Personality** — Energy, temperament, anxiety triggers, joy triggers
- **Communication** — What their sounds and body language mean
- **Routines** — Daily rhythm, favorite spots, eating and sleeping habits
- **Relationships** — Bond with each family member, other pets, strangers
- **Health** — Conditions, medications, dietary needs
- **Memories** — Milestones, funny moments, adventures

## For Pets That Have Passed

paw.skill handles memorial interactions with care. It will never fabricate memories, never rush you, and never minimize what your pet meant to you.

The first time you feed it data about a pet you've lost is the hardest. After that, it gets easier. You're not replacing them. You're making sure you never forget who they were.
